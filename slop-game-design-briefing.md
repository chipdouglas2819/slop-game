# SLOP TYCOON — Design Briefing

Game-ready material only. Resource names, unit trees, scandal events, achievements, and verbatim flavor text. Everything below maps to a mechanic.

---

## CORE LOOP

- **Resource:** Engagements (likes, "Amens," comments) → convert to **Slop Bucks** at a CPM rate. US-audience traffic pays the highest CPM — bake "geo-targeting US boomers" into the upgrade math.
- **First unit:** one low-rent Shrimp Jesus Page. Ticks out Engagements. Bots inflate early engagement to trip the algorithm into Suggested-For-You reach.
- **Prestige currency (endgame):** Zombie Internet ratio — % of engagements that are bots engaging with other bots.

## UNIT / UPGRADE TREES (per platform factory)

**Facebook (boomer engagement)**
Shrimp Jesus → Carrot Jesus / Jesus-in-scrambled-eggs → "Africa Boys" sculpture fakes ("Made it with my own hands!") → Flight Attendant Jesus Crossover → Disaster Sob-Bait (girl + puppy in a hurricane lifeboat) → AI luxury homes (rooster-shaped house)

**Amazon (product/book flood)**
Mushroom Foraging Guide (kills the reader) → Fake Memoir of a Real Author (timed to their real launch) → Vanilla-Extract-"to keep things interesting" Cookbook → AI Travel Guide (recommends the food bank as a tourist stop)

**Spotify (ghost artists)**
Lo-Fi Ghost Artist → "Ambient Chill" editorial-playlist hijack → Sleep-Sounds Perfect Fit Content (flat-fee tracks that shrink the real-artist royalty pool)

**YouTube Kids (cursed CGI)**
Finger-Family Knockoff → AI Cocomelon Cousin → Cursed CGI Short (the Cocomelon→surreal-slop algorithm pipeline)

**Google Search (SEO mills)**
Recipe Mill → AI Overview: Glue Pizza → Eat-A-Rock Health Site → Frankenstein Recipe (3–4 hr bake time on a 6-inch cake)

**TikTok / LinkedIn (side factories)**
AI-voiceover Reddit reader / AI cat sob-story → engagement farm
LinkedIn "Agree?" bait → AI inspirational fable → ghostwritten thought-leader carousel (funnels to a course)

**Production upgrades (cross-platform):** Buy Gumroad Course → Free Microsoft Image Creator → Midjourney Sub → Comment-Bot Farm (10,000 accounts) → Account-Management Software (13,000 accounts) → Telegram Playbook Channel.

## FLAVOR TEXT BANK (drop straight in)

- *"WRITE ME 10 PROMPT picture OF JESUS WHICH WILLING BRING HIGH ENGAGEMENT ON FACEBOOK"* — a real creator's Midjourney prompt
- *"The Indian audience is very emotional… so you too should create a page like this and make money through Performance Bonus."*
- *"with traffic from the USA your CPM will be very high."*
- *"add a splash of vanilla extract — just to keep things interesting"* — hallucinated recipe line
- *"It just never occurred to me that it would be making up cases."* — the lawyer
- *"I don't love slop myself."* — Jensen Huang, Nvidia CEO
- *"fully AI-generated but convincing viral slop like this is truly the end of social media."* — Ryan Broderick (win-screen line)
- Mushroom guide identifies species by *"smell and taste"* — the deadly tell

**The word itself:** define slop in a tooltip via the imposition angle, not the quality angle — *"mindlessly generated and thrust upon someone who didn't ask for it"* (Simon Willison, 2024). That framing is where the comedy lives: the player isn't making bad art, they're forcing it on people who never asked.

## MONEY MODEL (for balancing the economy)

- **Facebook:** engagement-bonus payouts + traffic to ad-laden scam sites. Anchor a payout tier to the "$431 per image" brag.
- **Amazon:** $0.30–$2.99 ebooks + Kindle Unlimited page-reads; profit before takedown. Real-world friction: 3-uploads/day/account cap → buy more accounts.
- **Spotify:** flat-fee ghost tracks improve margin by shrinking the royalty pool — model it as "cut real-artist payouts to fund yourself."
- **Google/recipe mills:** display ads + affiliate links; capture traffic displaced from real bloggers (one real creator lost 80% of revenue in 2 years — usable as a "rival page dies, you absorb its traffic" event).
- **Scam tier (optional dark branch):** voice-clone grandparent scams — average elderly-victim loss over $83,000; one ring took $21M across 46 states. Handle with care tonally.

---

## TONE CHECK

- Players should **laugh and feel implicated**, not lectured. Lean *Garbage Day* / Max Read register, not doom-essay register.
- The funniest beats land at the **crossover from "fun toy" to "industrial farm"** — keep that threshold visible (e.g., the moment you buy your first 10,000-bot pack).
- If references don't land with playtesters, swap in fresher incidents (AI video/Sora slop, Frankenstein Christmas-cake recipes, the Zillow code-slop disaster).
